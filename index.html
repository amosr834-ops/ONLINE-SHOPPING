Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Woven for Today

WeavedNest was born from a passion for preserving ancient weaving traditions. Our baskets are handcrafted by artisan families in rural communities, using techniques passed down over generations.

Every purchase directly supports the artisans who make them, ensuring fair wages and sustainable livelihoods. When you buy a WeavedNest basket, you're not just getting a beautiful object — you're keeping a tradition alive.
